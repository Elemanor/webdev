## The Science of Stack Effect

### Fundamental Physics

Stack effect occurs due to air density differences caused by temperature:

**Pressure Difference Equation:**

Where:
- ŒîP = pressure difference (Pa)
- C = 0.0342 (constant)
- œÅ = outdoor air density
- g = gravity (9.81 m/s¬≤)
- h = height (m)
