Common causes of basement flooding in Toronto include:

  • Spring Thaw: Melting snow and frozen ground prevent proper drainage
  • Heavy Rainfall: Toronto's storm systems can overwhelm drainage
  • Foundation Cracks: Allow water seepage through walls and floors
  • Poor Grading: Water flows toward instead of away from foundation
  • Sump Pump Failure: Power outages or mechanical failure during storms
  • Sewer Backup: City sewer systems overwhelmed during heavy rain

Interior Waterproofing:

  • Manages water that enters the basement
  • Includes drainage systems, sump pumps, vapor barriers
  • Less expensive ($3,000-8,000)
  • Minimal excavation required

Exterior Waterproofing:

  • Prevents water from reaching foundation walls
  • Includes membrane installation, drainage, grading
  • More comprehensive ($8,000-15,000)
  • Requires excavation around foundation

We often recommend a combination approach for maximum protection with our lifetime warranty.
