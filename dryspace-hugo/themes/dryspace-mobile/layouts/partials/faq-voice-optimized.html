Cost & Pricing

How much does basement waterproofing cost in Toronto?

Basement waterproofing in Toronto costs between $3,000 and $15,000, depending on the method:

  • Interior waterproofing: $3,000 to $7,000
  • Exterior waterproofing: $8,000 to $15,000
  • Crack injection: $500 to $1,000 per crack
  • French drain installation: $4,000 to $8,000

These prices include materials, labor, and warranty. Toronto homeowners can save up to $3,400 with city rebates for sump pumps and backwater valves.

Is waterproofing worth it for my Toronto home?

Yes, waterproofing is worth it. It typically increases your Toronto home's value by 5-10% and prevents damage that can cost $10,000-$50,000 to repair. Most importantly, it protects your family's health by preventing mold growth.

Process & Timeline

How long does basement waterproofing take?

Basement waterproofing typically takes:

  • Interior waterproofing: 2-3 days
  • Exterior waterproofing: 5-7 days
  • Crack injection: 2-4 hours per crack
  • Emergency repairs: Same day service available

Weather conditions and the size of your Toronto home can affect timeline.

Can I stay in my home during waterproofing?

Yes, you can stay in your home during waterproofing. Interior work is contained to the basement, and we use dust barriers. Exterior work happens outside. Most Toronto families continue their normal routines during the process.

Technical Questions

What's the difference between interior and exterior waterproofing?

The main differences are:

Interior Waterproofing
  • Manages water that enters
  • Less expensive ($3,000-$7,000)
  • No excavation needed
  • Completed in 2-3 days
  • Good for finished basements
Exterior Waterproofing
  • Prevents water from entering
  • More expensive ($8,000-$15,000)
  • Requires excavation
  • Takes 5-7 days
  • Most comprehensive solution

How long does waterproofing last?

Professional waterproofing lasts 20-40 years. Interior systems typically last 20-30 years, while exterior waterproofing can last 25-40 years. Quality installation and materials make the difference. We offer lifetime transferable warranties for peace of mind.

Toronto-Specific

Why do Toronto homes have so many basement water problems?

Toronto homes face unique water challenges due to:

  • Clay soil that expands and contracts with moisture
  • High water table in many neighborhoods
  • Aging infrastructure in older areas like The Annex
  • Extreme weather with freeze-thaw cycles
  • Underground streams like the buried Garrison Creek

Does the City of Toronto offer waterproofing rebates?

Yes, Toronto offers up to $3,400 in rebates through the Basement Flooding Protection Subsidy Program:

  • $1,750 for a backwater valve
  • $1,750 for a sump pump
  • $500 for storm sewer disconnection

We help all our customers apply for these rebates.

Emergency Questions

What should I do if my basement is flooding right now?

If your basement is flooding:

  1. Turn off electricity to the basement at the breaker
  2. Stop the water source if possible (shut off main valve)
  3. Call us immediately at (437) 545-0067
  4. Document damage with photos for insurance
  5. Don't enter deep water - wait for professionals

We provide 24/7 emergency service and typically arrive within 2 hours in Toronto.
