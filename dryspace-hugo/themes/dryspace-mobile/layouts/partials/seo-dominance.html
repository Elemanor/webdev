Frequently Asked Questions About Basement Waterproofing

Common signs you need basement waterproofing include:

  • Water stains or dampness on walls or floors
  • Musty odors or mold growth
  • Efflorescence (white powder) on concrete
  • Cracks in foundation walls or floor
  • Pooling water or flooding after rain

If you notice any of these signs, contact Dryspace for a free inspection at (437) 545-0067.
